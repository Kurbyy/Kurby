Services

Full keyboard build $60 - I will put together all the parts and mod the stabalizers and solder switches into pcb if needed. All parts for build must be provided by customer.

Switch Lubing/Filming $30-$40 - I have modded over 500 switches so rest assured your are getting a good service. I will take apart and lube and film each switch and make sure they are in working order after they have all been modded. Lube will be included in service but if you wish to have switches filmed they will have to be provided by customer.

Desoldering Switches $20 - If your in need of desoldering some switches on one of your keyboards and dont have the means to do so. I will cleanly desolder each switch and make sure to not cause any damage to pcb or switches.
